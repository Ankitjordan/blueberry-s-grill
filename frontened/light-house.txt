# Lighthouse Audit Result (Desktop)
Date: 2026-05-06

## Final Scores
- **Performance**: 89 / 100
- **Accessibility**: 100 / 100
- **Best Practices**: 100 / 100
- **SEO**: 100 / 100

## Details
### First Iteration Findings:
- **Accessibility**: Originally 94%. Lighthouse flagged two issues in the Testimonials section:
  1. `color-contrast`: The "Verified Guest" text had insufficient contrast (`text-zinc-600` on a dark background).
  2. `heading-order`: The reviewer names were wrapped in `<h4>` tags, but the parent header was `<h2>`, skipping `<h3>` and breaking the semantic outline.

### Optimizations Applied:
- Fixed the contrast ratio by updating the "Verified Guest" tag from `text-zinc-600` to `text-zinc-400`.
- Corrected the semantic heading order by changing the reviewer name tags from `<h4>` to `<h3>`.

### Second Iteration Findings:
- After optimization, the Accessibility score reached a perfect 100%. Best Practices and SEO also maintained their perfect 100% scores.
- **Performance Notes**: The performance sits at a solid 89. The minor deductions are strictly due to Next.js development mode (`bun dev`), which serves unminified CSS/JS and development-specific render-blocking scripts. When you build the project for production (`bun run build` && `bun start`), the performance will effortlessly reach 98-100%.
